Question: What is the best time to water plants?

Response A (Midday): Incorrect. Watering at midday can lead to quick evaporation and less water absorption.

Back to Question

Response B (Morning): Correct! Morning is the best time to water plants, allowing water to soak into the soil before the heat of the day.

Back to Question

Response C (Evening): Incorrect. Evening watering can lead to mold growth due to cooler temperatures.
